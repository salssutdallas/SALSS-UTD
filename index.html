Student Association of Lean Six Sigma

Welcome to UT Dallas Lean Six Sigma

Student Association of Lean Six Sigma (SALSS) works to set up a community of Lean Six Sigma Professionals.

Our Mission

The mission for SALSS is to help our members in their careers by enhancing their professional qualifications. Students will learn how to apply, manage, and deploy Lean Six Sigma principles and thinking. We aim to build a positive relationship with students, UT Dallas faculty, and Dallas Fort Worth business Professionals to promote the growth of Lean Six Sigma practices.

Our Goals

We seek to educate members on career opportunities, promote the understanding and practices of Lean Six Sigma and encourage members to get certified in Lean Six Sigma.

Established in 2017

The Student Association of Lean Six Sigma (SALSS) at The University of Texas at Dallas is a student-led organization. Its membership is open to all students at The University of Texas at Dallas.
